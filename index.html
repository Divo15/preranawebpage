Prerna Educational and Charitable Trust, established in 2013, is a non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare of orphans and visually impaired individuals. Our mission is to provide love, care and education to those in need, helping them live with dignity and achieve their fullest potential.

Join us in making a difference in the lives of the less fortunate. Together, we can inspire hope and create a better tomorrow for the orphaned and visually impaired.

Prerna Educational and Charitable Trust was founded in 2013 and lead by Sri.Vaibhav Biradar, with a vision to support and uplift the orphaned and visually impaired members of society. Since its inception, the trust has grown into a beacon of hope for countless individuals, providing them with shelter, education, and a path toward independence.
